Things to do in Lake Louise

Hiking Trails

Lake Louise serves as a hub for numerous hiking trails catering to all levels of hikers. The Lake Louise Lakeshore Trail is an easy walk suitable for families, while more adventurous hikers can tackle trails like the Plain of Six Glaciers or the Lake Agnes Tea House trail, which reward hikers with panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and glaciers.

Canoeing

You can rent canoes from the boathouse at Lake Louise and paddle on the crystal-clear waters of the lake. It's a serene and picturesque way to experience the lake and its surroundings. Canoe rentals are available during the summer months.

Lake Louise Gondola

Located nearby, the Lake Louise Gondola offers breathtaking views of the valley and surrounding mountains. It's a great way to get an elevated perspective of the area's beauty. In the summer, you can also explore hiking trails and enjoy wildlife viewing at the top.
